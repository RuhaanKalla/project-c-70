Campus

Smart Classrooms



Every classroom is spacious and aesthetically designed to provide luxury seating so that each student is comfortable. This has a twofold benefit. One, it helps the teachers to communicate effectively to all the students and the other advantage is that students are provided individual attention.



School Library



The school library classified as reading room (Classes I & II), junior library (III to V) and senior library (VI onwards) has a comprehensive collection of books which are suitable for that age group and requirement. The vast collection ranges from story books to academic and reference books as well as a wide range of journals and periodicals. Besides, there is adequate reference material, educational CDs, newspapers and magazines on varied subjects. The ambience of the library offers a perfect place for serious study as well as a peaceful place for light reading and distressing. The computers installed in the library, allow the children to access reference material online through the week. The books are managed by a qualified librarian who assists the children in the selection of books appropriate for their age.



Auditorium



The new state-of-the-art auditorium of AIS Gurgaon - 46, is well equipped with all major technological equipments to convene functions of different kinds. Centrally air conditioned, it is the perfect space to hold assemblies of large capacities. The stage of the auditorium has been tactfully designed, to host theatrical and dramatics performances successfully. Great emphasis is laid on enhancing the confidence of students on a regular basis.



Sports & Games



Sports forms an integral part of the school curriculum and hence, the school campus is designed to provide ample space for conducting various indoor and outdoor sports activities. The school has all the facilities required for national level players, to motivate them to excel and perform. The school has specific coaches for different sports who are available during school hours as well as post school, to provide expert training in chess, taekwondo, cricket, hockey, table tennis, skating, basketball, volleyball and yoga. Special summer camps are organised to expose the students to team building and exciting sports activities, thus helping them to conquer challenges and emerge as winners.It is the constant endeavour of the school to inspire more and more students to take part in various games.


Learning Enhancement Centre(LEC)



Learning Enhancement Centre (LEC) at Amity International School, Sector 46 follows an inclusive approach for children with special needs. It provides full-fledged support system, catering to the needs of students who are Slow Learners or have learning disabilities, Attention Deficit Hyper Activity Disorder (ADHD)& Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). In addition, we guide students who have difficulties in learning or are underachievers in their core subjects. LEC provides support to students who are academically bright. Students with these kinds of special needs are likely to benefit from additional educational services such as specialized approach towards learning process, use of technology, a specifically adapted teaching learning environment, or resource room aimed at enhancing the potential of such children. We have a team of trained Special Educators who firmly believe and practice the theory of Multiple Intelligence to fill in the learning gaps, boost their confidence and make them independent learners. Ramanujan 2019

Winning Math fun activity' for Class IV-V and ‘Problem solving' for Class IX-X in ramanujan 2019.

School team bagged the second position in both ‘Math fun activity' for Class IV-V and ‘Problem solving' for Class IX-X in the 19th edition of Ramanujan. The annual inter school competition of math was hosted by AIS Noida and more than 250 students from 29 schools of Delhi / NCR participated in it.

Whats New

  • Virtual Teacher's Day

    Senior school council members celebrated the first ever virtual Teachers’ Day with a special online assembly. They sang songs, recited poems and conducted online fun and games activities. The event was graced by Dr Ashok K. Chauhan, Founder President, Amity Universe and Dr (Mrs) Amita Chauhan, Chairperson, Amity Group of Schools and RBEF, both of whom appreciated teachers for their relentless hardwork and constant innovation. Around 1700 educators from different branches of Amity Group of Schools also joined the special online session.

  • HERITAGE DIARIES

    A virtual class presentation titled ‘Dharo Darpan- from Heritage Diaries’ was held for Classes III-IV. Students showcased rich and diverse heritage and culture of the states of Telangana, Haryana, Maharashtra, West Bengal, Tamil Nadu and Rajasthan through folk dances like Kalbelia, Ghoomar, Lavani and Mayilattam. They also enacted as famous personalities and shared the customs, cuisines, traditions and festivals of these states.

  • VIRTUAL LITERATI 2020

    Tenth and first ever virtual annual ‘Literati – A festival of Langauges’ was held by the school and the event was graced by Dr (Mrs) Amita Chauhan, Chairperson, Amity Group of Schools and RBEF. Around 48 schools with over 650 students all over India participated in the interschool event comprising competitions like debate, poem recitation, role plays, voicing opinions and quiz in 5 different languages being held across the week.The jury comprised eminent personas from various fields as well as Amity alumni.
